If you are on vacation in the UK the Vodafone TopUp And Go offer is a great way to stay online without the tie-in of a contract. The Huawei USB dongle works great with Ubuntu (10.04). The only problem is that the network manager wizard configures the wrong APN for TopUp And Go. The correct APN is ppbundle.internet.
